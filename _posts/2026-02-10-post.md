 Manakongtreecheep, K., Ctortecka, C., et al. developed a genetically encoded peptide library system, termed Pepyrus, that enables high-throughput identification of rare HLA-bound peptides. They designed an oligo pool in which the peptide library is conjugated with a Strep affinity-tagged Smt3, a yeast small ubiquitin-related modifier (SUMO) protein to facilitate high-throughput SUMO–peptide fusion protein purification, using a Ulp1 cleavage site.
